About Candie's Photo Frames

At Candie's Photo Frames, we know that a picture is worth a thousand words, and the right frame turns it into a lasting story. Our mission is simple: to offer beautifully crafted photo frames that don't just hold your memories but elevate them. We combine style, quality, and affordability to create frames that fit perfectly into your home and life.

Our Values

Behind every frame is a belief that memories deserve the best. From the materials we source to the designs we create, these core values guide everything we do:

  • Quality Craftsmanship: Each frame is built with care, ensuring it lasts as long as the memories it holds.
  • Customer Satisfaction: We’re not happy until you’re happy – whether it’s our service or our product, we aim to exceed expectations.
  • Customization Options: We understand every home and style is unique, which is why we offer personalized frame designs that speak to your individuality.
  • Sustainability: We care about the environment, which is why we source eco-friendly materials and focus on minimizing our carbon footprint.

Meet Our Team

Behind Candie's Photo Frames is a passionate team dedicated to bringing your memories to life. We may be small, but our vision is big – and we’re growing. Here’s a little bit about the heart and soul behind our brand.

Candie Brown

Founder and Lead Designer

Candie’s love for photography and design led her to start this business. She believes in the power of creativity, and her goal is to bring joy to people by creating frames that complement and enhance the beauty of their captured moments.

Our Process

We’re passionate about quality, and our process reflects that. From hand-picking the finest materials to carefully crafting every frame, our approach ensures each product is built to last. Every frame is tested for durability and designed with attention to detail, so you can focus on what matters most – the memories within.
